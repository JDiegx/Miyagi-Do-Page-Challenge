About us

Welcome to Miyagi-Do Karate!

At Miyagi-Do Karate, we take pride in carrying on the legacy of the Great Master Miyagi and his unique approach to the art of karate. Founded with the purpose of teaching much more than mere self-defense techniques, our school focuses on the holistic development of our students: body, mind, and spirit.

Our Mission!

At Miyagi-Do, our mission is to provide a welcoming and safe environment where students of all ages can learn and grow in the art of karate. We strive to instill values such as respect, discipline, perseverance, and compassion in each of our students, not only as karate practitioners but also as individuals in society.

Our Approach:

We differentiate ourselves by focusing on the balance between body and mind. Our highly skilled instructors not only teach effective karate techniques but also guide students in developing skills such as concentration, emotional control, and self-confidence. We believe that true mastery of karate goes beyond physical strength; it is about cultivating inner strength and wisdom.

Our Community:

At Miyagi-Do, we value the importance of community and fellowship. We foster an atmosphere of mutual support where students help each other achieve their goals and celebrate each other's accomplishments. In addition to our regular karate classes, we organize social events, charitable activities, and internal competitions to strengthen the bonds within our community.

Join Us:

If you're looking for a place where you can learn karate while becoming the best version of yourself, Miyagi-Do Karate is the place for you! Whether you're an absolute beginner or an experienced practitioner, everyone is welcome in our Miyagi-Do family.

We look forward to seeing you soon at the dojo!
